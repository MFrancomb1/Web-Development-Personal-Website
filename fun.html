Skiing!

In the winter time you can find me outside playing in the snow. I help to run a small ski school on the Como Park Golf Course every weekend in January. We teach the very little ones, ages 4 and 5, to adult beginners, even some aspiring ski racers. This is a photo of me on the last da of classes, the kids always et a kick out of the costumes!
